# 1GAM January: Battle Pong
## Sun Jan 07 2018

So I've always wanted to make a game. That's how I started getting into programming in the first place. I would make little games on my TI-83+ calculator when I was supposed to be learning Geography or something like that. However, I tend to overcomplicate things and then get bored when I don't make much progress. So this year, I decided to unofficially participate in One Game a Month (1GAM).

I don't plan to make a [1GAM account](http://www.onegameamonth.com/) or anything, but I do want to make little games or prototypes and then post about them here. They will probably mostly be JS/canvas based, but I may dip into Game Maker or Unity or something. If they are made in JS, I will embed them inline. If they use some sort of external program, I will upload and host the files along with screenshots/gifs.

These won't be pretty, and the code will probably be messy and inefficient, but they should at least be playable. I will probably utilize a lot of ES6 features and only test on the latest stable Chrome, so your mileage my vary.

So without further ado, here is my January creation: Battle Pong.
